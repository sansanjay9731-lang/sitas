Founded with a passion for transforming beauty visions into reality, Sita's Signature has grown from a bridal makeup studio into HSR Layout's most trusted beauty and fashion house. And we've styled 2000+ brides for their most special day — each one a unique masterpiece. Every service we offer is crafted with premium international products (MAC, Bobbi Brown, Charlotte Tilbury) and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does bridal makeup cost at Sita's Signature? +

Our bridal makeup packages are customized based on the services you need. We offer complete wedding packages that cover engagement, haldi, mehndi, sangeet, and the wedding day — as well as individual event pricing. Every package includes premium international products (MAC, Bobbi Brown, Charlotte Tilbury), a trial session, and touch-up kit. Contact us at +91 98765 43210 for a personalized quote.

Do you provide on-location bridal makeup in Bangalore? +

Yes! We offer on-location bridal makeup services across all of Bangalore — HSR Layout, Koramangala, Whitefield, Electronic City, Jayanagar, Indiranagar, JP Nagar, and more. We also travel for destination weddings in Mysore, Coorg, Nandi Hills, and other Karnataka locations. Our team arrives well in advance to ensure a stress-free experience.

Can I rent a bridal lehenga from your collection? +

Absolutely! We have 200+ designer bridal lehengas, sarees, and Indo-Western outfits available for rent. Our collection includes pieces inspired by top designers. You can visit our HSR Layout studio for a trial session — we provide professional fitting and minor alterations to ensure the perfect look. Rental periods are flexible, and we handle dry cleaning. Walk-ins welcome Mon-Sat, 10AM-8PM.

What makeup courses do you offer at the academy? +

We offer four courses: Professional Makeup Artistry (3 months, full-time) covering HD airbrush, bridal, editorial, color theory, and portfolio building. Fashion Design & Styling (6 months) with illustration, draping, trend forecasting, and internship placement. Bridal Specialist (6 weeks, weekends) for working professionals. Self-Makeup Masterclass (2 weeks) for personal grooming. All courses include hands-on practice with live models, industry-certified diplomas, and placement support.

How far in advance should I book my bridal makeup? +

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season (November–February). This gives us time for a trial session where we finalize your look, discuss your outfit and jewelry, and plan the timeline for all wedding events. For destination weddings, 4-6 months advance booking is ideal. Early booking also ensures your preferred date is secured.

What is the difference between HD and airbrush bridal makeup? +

HD (High Definition) makeup uses finely milled pigments that look flawless under high-resolution photography and video — ideal for brides who want a natural, skin-like finish. Airbrush makeup is spray-applied using a compressor, creating an ultra-smooth, long-lasting, waterproof base that's perfect for outdoor weddings and hot weather. At Sita's Signature, we're experts in both techniques and recommend the best option based on your wedding venue, weather, and personal preference during your trial session.
